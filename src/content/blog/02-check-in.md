- Access is via a lockbox with a numerical combination near the main entrance.  
- When facing the glass entrance door, look to the arched window on the right.  
- The key box is located at the bottom left corner of this window.  

### Note

The code to open the lockbox is provided via Whatsapp or email before your arrival.
